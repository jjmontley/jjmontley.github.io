We want our cooking to remind people of their grandma's cooking. We provide catering for the biggest events or just trays for you and your family on a Sunday night. We are very creative with unqiue, delicious tasting food and sweets.

Our cookies are a favorite for many. We first started of very small and just trying out different flavors. We came up with such flavors as banana pudding, which many people recommend, rum, crown royal, red velvet, peanut butter and also the old fashioned chocolate chip, oatmeal raisin, and butter.

After the cookies, we then moved on to other sweets such as cheesecakes, cakes, peach cobbler, and pie. Many started asking about catering main dishes for Sunday dinner, dinner parties, Thanksgiving, and also Christmas. Catering became a regular as more people began to order.
